Our Story

I started my brand in 2012, around 12 years ago, after I gave up on my damaged hair caused by unknown chemicals used in salons. They even told me it was due to the evil eye. Determined to find a solution, I researched hair oils and developed my first mixture. After numerous adjustments, I finally restored my hair after about a year, as my roots had almost disappeared. During this journey, an idea began to take shape, and I decided to bring it to life.
